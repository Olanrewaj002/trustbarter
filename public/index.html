Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this platform?

Anyone can use this platform as long as you have an item to barter for goods or services, or outright sale.

How much will I pay to use this platform?

We charge a 5% fee known as service charge on every successful transaction using this platform.

How do you measure service charge?

5% of the value of an item being sold or bartered on this platform is called service charge.

How fast can I barter or sell my item?

Items that are in high demand gets sold or bartered in as quickly as 24/48hrs on this platform.
